KAELEN STONEFANG

Lord-Relictor | Monster-Hunter

Basic Traits

Nickname: "First Fang" (Mortal title), "Stonefang"

Sex: Male

Race: Stormcast Eternal (Human, Reforged)

Height: Immense, even for a Stormcast.

Body Type: Heavy, powerful, and muscular.

Eyes: Pale and watchful.

Skin: Weathered, tan.

Hair: Dark brown or black, likely greying.

Birthplace: Foothills of the Jagged Spine mountains, Ghur.

Religion

Loyal to Sigmar, but his primal Ghurish spirit chafes against the rigid doctrine. He views Sigmar's power as a raw, natural storm rather than a civilized faith.

Aethyric Connection

Channels the power of the Celestial Storm (Azyr), but his connection is filtered through his primal Ghurish spirit. His magic feels less like divine light and more like a raw, mountain thunderstorm—a force of nature, not faith.

Character Traits

Fatal Flaw: Too savage; he *enjoys* the slaughter and the hunt more than a holy warrior should.

Fears: Losing the last bits of his mortal self with every reforging, becoming nothing but a mindless, golden tool.

Ambition: To hunt the unnatural taints of Chaos and protect his 'pack'.

Ideals: The primal covenant of the hunt (kill or be killed); a fierce, animalistic need to protect his "pack."

Strengths: *Hunter's Instinct.* Kaelen's senses are supernaturally sharp. He can track a foe across realms, sense the "taint" of Chaos, and often predicts an enemy's movements on pure instinct. Adept at felling beasts of any size.

Weaknesses: Suspicious of 'civilized' folk; is, in turn, viewed with suspicion by his brethren.

Combat Skills

Prayers of the Storm: Can call down lightning or wreathe his allies in storm-auras. Protecting them from enemy magics, blades and even empowering their own strikes.

Binding and Banishment: His primary duty. He excels at forcing malign spirits, daemons, and echoes of the slain to depart the Mortal Realms, often in smokes of blinding primal lightning strikes. This also applies to binding his own allies’ souls to his Reliquary.

Cauterizing Bolt: Kaelen's "healing" is a primal, violent act. He calls down a targeted, agonizing jolt of raw lightning, not to soothe, but to *cauterize*. The bolt sears wounds shut, burns out corruption, and shocks the ally's spirit back to full fighting strength.

Equipment

Relic Hammer: A potent holy weapon. Kaelen wields his with savage force, treating it less like a symbol of faith and more like a primal, bone-shattering club.

Heavy-bladed Storm-glaive: Often magnetically clasped to his armor's pack. He uses it as a hunter's spear for powerful, gutting thrusts and heavy, severing blows against larger beasts.

Ghurish Hatchet: A heavy-bladed hatchet (reminiscent of his mortal weapon) carried at his hip for close-quarters work.

Armor: Mortis Armor, heavily scarred from countless battles. It is adorned with trophies: beast fangs, thick pelts, and totem-like carvings.

Icon: A back-mounted **Reliquary Icon** (containing holy relics or bones to channel his power).

Important Item: A single, massive, Slaanesh-tainted fang from the Chimera that killed him in his mortal life. He has fastened it to his shoulder pauldron as a reminder of his death and purpose.

Appearance

Backstory

In the savage foothills of the Jagged Spine mountains in Ghur, Kaelen was not a soldier or a king; he was the First Fang of the Cliff-Eater tribe. His life was a brutal, relentless cycle of hunt, feast, and survival. He led the hunting parties that brought down carnosaurian tyrants and rock-skinned behemoths, providing the bone, meat, and hide his people needed to see the next sunrise. He was a creature of pure instinct, bound not by laws but by the primal covenant of the hunt: kill, or be killed and feed the next generation. He respected the great beasts he hunted, seeing their strength as a mirror of his own, and despised the creeping taint of Chaos that warped them into unnatural horrors.

His death was not a glorious last stand against a gleaming champion of the Dark Gods. It was a bloody, desperate, and muddy affair. A warherd of Gors, led by a Slaaneshi-tainted Chimera, descended on his tribe's cavern-enclave during the height of the monsoon season. Kaelen and his hunters met them in the rain-slicked gorge that served as their only entrance. He fought with spear and hatchet, his howls matching the braying of the beastmen, and ultimately buried his hatchet in the Chimera's skull. But he was overwhelmed by sheer numbers, dying with his teeth locked in a Gor's throat as a dozen spears pierced his back.

Sigmar plucked that raw, furious spirit from the ether just as it was being dragged toward the insatiable maws of the Chaos Gods. The reforging was agony. It felt like being caged, like having his fangs filed down and his instincts smothered in celestial light and holy purpose. He emerged as Kaelen Stonefang, a Lord-Relictor in the Astral Templars. His innate, primal connection to the spirits of Ghur was reforged into a divine mandate to bind and banish. The savage joy of the hunt was replaced by the cold, righteous fury of the storm, but the change was imperfect.

Now, Stonefang serves the God-King, but the beast within him chafes against the holy armor. He is a loyal and devastatingly effective weapon for Sigmar, especially when unleashed in his native Realm of Ghur, where he moves like a ghost from his own past. But his fellow Stormcast eyed him with suspicion. He seems to *enjoy* the slaughter too much, and the prayers he intones to bind malign spirits sound more like the growling challenges of a predator than a litany of faith. Kaelen fears the day the beast breaks its cage, but he fears something more: that with every reforging, another piece of the man he was is chipped away, leaving only the cold, golden, mindless *tool*.
